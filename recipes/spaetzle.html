Spaetzle

Description

Easy recipe for delicious home made spaetzle.
If you've got any taste, you're gonna love this one.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot of water to boiling
  2. Salt water
  3. Combine flour and water until mixture is a wet batter
  4. Use spaetzle maker to drop batter into water
