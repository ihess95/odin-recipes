Spaetzle

Description

Easy recipe for delicious home made spaetzle.

If you've got any taste, you're gonna love this one.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unpasteurized cream

Steps

  1. Put cream in a sealable container
  2. Shake container until cream solidifies and whey separates
  3. Pour out liquid
  4. Enjoy
