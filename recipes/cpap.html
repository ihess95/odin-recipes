Chicken Paprikash

Description

This is a classic hungarian recipe that I prefer served over spaetzle. It is a very simple recipe that is very tasty. A tomato and pepper based sauce accompanies braised chicken.
If you've got any taste, you're gonna love this one.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup paprika
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup onions
  • 1 Anaheim or other mild pepper
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup garlic
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup pepper

Steps

  1. Chop your veggies.
  2. Heat your pan to medium high, add oil
  3. Season your chicken with salt and pepper
  4. Brown Chicken
  5. Remove chicken and add onions
  6. Add peppers and garlic and saute
  7. Add tomato paste and paprikas
  8. Add chicken and liquid and simmer for 30 mintues
  9. Add sour cream and simmer for 10 minutes
  10. Serve over spaetzle
